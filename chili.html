Cole’s Chili

  • 6 servings
  • 45 minutes
  • Cole Goins
  • Vegetarian

A hearty chili - no meat needed.

  • 30 oz 2 cans of beans, drained - black and kidney

  • ½ package spinach

  • 3 tsp cumin

Ingredients

  • 2 tbsp olive oil

Heat olive oil over medium-high in a large pot or saucepan. Add onions and garlic and saute until translucent, about 3 minutes.
