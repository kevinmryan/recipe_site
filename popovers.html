Papa’s Pop Overs

  • 3 servings
  • 45 minutes
  • Bee Plant
  • Vegetarian

Kevin’s favorite breakfast.

  • 3 eggs, beaten
  • 1 ½ cup milk
  • 1 ½ cup sifted flour
  • ½ tsp salt

Ingredients

Grease the muffin pans.

  • 3 eggs, beaten
  • 1 ½ cup milk
  • 1 ½ cup sifted flour
  • ½ tsp salt

In a bowl, mix eggs, milk, flower, and salt. Lightly beat the ingredients together.

Fill cupcake tins almost ¾ full.

Start with oven cold, set oven to 425-450 °F and bake for 30 minutes.

Enjoy!
